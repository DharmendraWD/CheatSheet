Face Swap:

1. Select the face with lasso tool. (Do not select exactly all part, just select from eyelash to down lips), then press CTRL+J to copy.
2. Now select the selected face layer and decrease the opacity, so you can see backward face.
3. Press CTRL+T  and adjust the face, then increase the opacity.
4. Right click to the layer thumbnail select "Select Pixels", hide the
copied face and right click on background then go to Select > Modify > Contrast
and then press OK.

5. Now, hit delete key, and unhide the copied face.
6. Go to Edit > Auto Blend Layer > Stack Image. ( Tick Seamless.. Tick 
Content Aware ....) hit OK.
