Skin Retouching:

1: Duplicate the layer, change the first's layer Blend mode to "Vivid Light".
2. Go to Image > Adjustments > Invert.
4. Go to Filter > Other > High Pass. (set High Pass radius as requirement)
5. Go to Filter > Blur > Gaussion Bur (set lower then High Pass).
6. Click Layer Mask thumbnail, CTRL+I to invert. 
7. Select Brush tool and now paint.
