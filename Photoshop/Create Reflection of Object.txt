Create Reflection of Object:

1. Duplicate the object, Right click on of them and select Flip Horizontal, then drag where you want the reflection. 
2. Now, right click on it and click on "Wrap", Make a little bit wrap. 
3. Now click on Layer Mask then "Gradient tool" then drag over the object. 
4. Then go to Filter > Blur > Gaussion Blur, set Blur hit OK.
5. Create new layer, then little brush on duplicated layer.
