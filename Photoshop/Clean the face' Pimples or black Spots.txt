Clean the face' Pimples or black Spots:

1. Duplicate the background layer. (CTRL+J)
2. Select Upper layer and Press CTRL+I to invert the layer.
3. Change blend mode normal to "Vivid light"
4. Now go to Filter> Other >High Pass. 
5. Increase the radius until the blemishes gets dissappear and then press OK.
6. Go to Filter > Blur > Gaussion Blur, increase the radius little bit, hit OK.
7. Now, Create a layer Mask and Press CTRL+I to invert the mask
8. Now, select the Brush, make sure that foreground color is white. 
9. Now start brushing over the Pimples or black spots.

Enjoy..
