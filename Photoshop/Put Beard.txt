Put Beard and Moustache:

1. Select the beard and Moustache from other image using Laso Too.
2. Copy the selected part and paste on the image where you want.
3. Change the blend mode to "Multiply" of Beard layer.
4. Go to Image > Adjustments > Desaturate 
5. Go to Image > Adjustment > Levels and slide the ruler. 
6. Create layer Mask and choose the brush and remove the unwanted parts. (Make sure Foreground color black, background White).
