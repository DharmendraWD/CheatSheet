Blur The Background:

1. Go to Select > Subject then duplicate the layer, right click on duplicate layer and select "select pixel".
2. Now, hide the upper layer and click on down layer (Original layer) and then go to Select > Modify > Expand, set the value 20 and hit OK.
3. Now go to Edit > FIll > Content Aware, tick "Color Adaption" option and Blending mode "Normal" hit OK.

4. Now unhide the upper layer then select down layer and go to Filter >Blur > gaussion Blur and set the blur then Hit OK.
