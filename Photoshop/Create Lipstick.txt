Create Lipstick:

1. Make solid color layer and select the color red. Change the 
layer Normal to Multiply.
2. Click on layer mask thumbnail, then Press CTRL+I, (Make the foreground color white) and paint on lips.

3. Click on fx down layer and choose "Blending Option" 
4. Set it according to Image.
5. Double clicko to Layer Mask and increase the feather like 9 to 10.
