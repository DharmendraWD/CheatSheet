Easily Change Hair Color:
1.Select hair carefully using (Quick selection) or any other tools.
2.Click on tab "Select > Modify > Contract. Keep the value 12 to 15 in "Contract by" option and hit OK.
3.Make a layer of solid color and choose the hair color and click OK.
4.Now, change the blend mode from "Normal" to "Soft" and click on masked icon of solid color layer and increase the "feather" 28 to 35 click OK.

5.Now, Decrease the Opacity as your requirement
