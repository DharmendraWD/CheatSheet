Set Text or Image on Curved Surface:

1. Select the curved surface using Quick selection tool and Create a new Layer using CTRL+J.
2. Select "Rectangle Tool" and draw a rectangle on curved surface.
3. Right click on Rectangle layer and click on "Convert to smart Object".
4. Decrease the opacity of Rectangle Layer, and press CTRL+T and choose "Distort".
5. Then fit the layer from up down side and If you are facing problem to adjust then right click on rectangle and click on "Wrap" then try.
