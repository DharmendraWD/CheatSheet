Brighten Specific Area:
1.Select the object using quick selection or any other which need to be brighten.
2.Create an " Exposure " adjustment layer, increase " Exposure and Gamma correction " (According to your requirement)
3.Double click on the Mask of Exposure layer.
4.Increase the feather 30 to 40px.

5.Create a " Curve " adjustment layer then hold ALT and click on " auto "
6.Click on " End dark and light color'
7.Click on " Highlight " option (bellow) and select white yellowish color. Click OK,YES

8.Then select " Curves " and "Exposure" layer and keep them in a group.
