Blend anything on Wall:

1. Select the object which you want to blend on wall, then Select "Rectangular Marquee Tool" and Draw a rectanle around the object, Press CTRL+X.
2. Go to Filter > Vanishing Point, then drwa the line(Box) as you want to blend the object on wall, then CTRL+V and then drag the object under the Box. then Hit OK

3. Select Object Layer and click on "fx" option under layer then choose "Blending option"
4. Now separate the little icon of "Underlying Layer" both black and white, then drag both for better result.
