Put Object on your Cloth:

1. Right click to the layer and sele Select Blending Option 
2. Break the "Underlying Layer" slider's icon.
(Set one Black slider on little away from black, one black little away from white, and one white exactly on white corner and one white little away from white)

3. Duplicate the original background layer and move it to top, right click and create "Clipping Mask", Change Blend mode from Normal to "Linear Burn".

4. Decrease the opacity.
5. go to Filter >Blur > Gaussion Blur. set as your requirement.
